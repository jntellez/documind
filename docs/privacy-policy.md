# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 26/05/2026

Documind is a mobile application that helps users save, read, organize, and interact with documents and web content.

## 1. Information We Collect

Documind may collect and process the following types of information:

### Account information

When you sign in, we may receive basic account information from authentication providers such as Google or GitHub, including your name, email address, and provider account identifier.

### User content

Documind processes content that you choose to provide, including:

- links submitted for reading or processing
- uploaded documents
- document titles, text, and related metadata
- chat questions and interactions related to your documents

### Local device storage

Documind may store certain information locally on your device to support offline access, cached documents, app preferences, and session persistence.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

- authenticate users
- save and organize documents
- process links and uploaded files
- provide reading and document chat features
- support offline access and synchronization
- improve app reliability and functionality

## 3. AI Processing

Some content you submit to Documind may be processed using AI-powered services in order to:

- analyze documents
- generate responses to document-related questions
- support search, summarization, or content understanding features

You should avoid submitting sensitive personal, confidential, or highly private information unless you are comfortable with that content being processed for these purposes.

## 4. Data Storage

Depending on the feature, data may be stored:

- locally on your device
- on Documind backend services
- in supporting infrastructure required to process and deliver app functionality

## 5. Authentication and Security

Documind uses third-party authentication providers such as Google and GitHub. Authentication tokens and session-related data may be stored securely on your device to keep you signed in.

We take reasonable steps to protect information, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

## 6. Data Sharing

Documind does not sell your personal information.

Data may be shared only when necessary to operate the app, including:

- authentication providers
- backend infrastructure
- AI or processing services used to power core features

## 7. Your Choices

You may choose not to sign in or not to upload content, but some app features may not work without that information.

You may also delete locally stored app data from your device, and you may request account deletion if that feature is available.

## 8. Children’s Privacy

Documind is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated over time. If changes are made, the updated version will be posted with a revised date.
